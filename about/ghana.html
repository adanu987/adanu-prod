The Republic of Ghana is a sovereign multinational state and unitary presidential constitutional democracy. Ghana is located along the Gulf of Guinea and Atlantic Ocean, in the subregion of West Africa.

Fun Fact

Ghanaians will always greet you by saying, “You are welcome.” It is genuinely lovely, and a beautiful representation of Ghana’s hospitable, welcoming culture.

Stat

Ghana is one of Africa’s most peaceful countries. According to the 2015 Global Peace Index, Ghana ranks 6th of the 44 countries in Africa.
