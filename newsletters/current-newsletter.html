We did it! 

Together we raised $14,600 in celebration of Adanu’s 20th Anniversary.  Funds came from cash donations and purchases from the April 2nd virtual auction and fundraising event. Add that to a generous supporter’s matching donation of $13,000 and we exceeded the goal to fund the Klefe Township school. Extra money will go toward the next school project.

Look for an update on these projects in future newsletters!

Thank you for your ongoing support of Adanu and its work empowering Volta Region communities.
