WE PUT POWER IN THE HANDS OF PEOPLE

Through Adanu, you have the unique opportunity to
partner with your friends and family to support and
invest in a specific community. In conjunction with our
volunteer program, any one of the three ideas below
provides you with the opportunity to really connect
and create lasting relationships with the people in the
community you support.

Note: You are not limited by the ideas listed below. We encourage creativity, and would love to hear about any other ideas you have to support our communities in Ghana!

Ways You Can Help

Host a Fundraiser

Do you have friends and family who you want to tell about Adanu, and encourage them to support our work financially or through a volunteer trip? We make it easy to host a fundraiser at your home, or even virtually.

Hold a Garage Sale

Are you looking for a way to get rid of your old stuff and make a difference? Hold a garage sale, invite your neighbors and friends to do the same, and donate your proceeds to a community project in Ghana.

Sponsor a Child

This program is currently being developed. If you have questions or want to help, email us today!.

the fundraiser kit

This downloadable kit provides all the information you need to host a successful fundraiser or hold a garage sale with your friends and neighbors. It is our hope that this kit will provide most of the support you need to make your fundraising efforts easy, fun and successful. We are excited to partner with you in supporting one of the many communities we have waiting!

The kit includes the video from our Approach page, a presentation agenda and script you can follow; donation forms; templates for invitations and thank you letters; tips for pre- and post-event communications; and much, much more!
